For many companies, communication between managers, employees, and volunteers has become a problem; oftentimes, there is a miscommunication and loss of information, especially between volunteer recruiters and volunteers. For companies who work off a volunteer basis, it can often be difficult to reach out to various volunteers even after communication is established. When recruiting new volunteers, sometimes volunteer rectruiters may miss crucial information such as phone numbers, job titkes, etc.; mishandled information can lead to loss of contact with volunteers, a crucial part to any organization/company.
